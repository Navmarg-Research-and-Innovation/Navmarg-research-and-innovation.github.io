THE MAGNETIC ARSENIC REMOVAL UNIT

One of our flagship solutions is the Magnetic Arsenic Removal Unit (MARU), which utilizes Molecular Magnetic Treatment of Arsenic in a sustainable and cost effective manner (Cost of Water per Litre: Nearly 0.1 Paise per litre ).
Highly scalable method suitable for any capacity, ensuring flexibility and design adaptability. High Flow Rate Technology for Arsenic Removal i.e. @800 LPH - 500ppb, @5000 LPH - 2000 (As per recorded during the Pilot Trial of Phase 2 and 1, 2021-2023).
Indian Patent -400769 IN, PCT (International) - WO 2021/1448

ADVANTAGES OF M.A.R.U

ZERO WASTAGE OF WATER

In our line filtration process, there's zero discharge of wastewater or brine during filtration. Experience eco-friendly filtration solutions.

NO ELECTRICITY REQUIRES

The convenience of our filtration process, where no electricity is required for the main filtration process.

LESS MAINTENANCE

Our low-maintenance filtration system. With a filter lifetime of up to 10 years and minimal backwash and cleaning requirements, you can trust in reliable performance for years to come.
