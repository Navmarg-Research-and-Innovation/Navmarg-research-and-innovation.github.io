METAL: Molecular Magnetic Technology for Arsenic Removal

Overview

At the core of our mission is a relentless drive for innovation to combat urgent environmental challenges. We harness state-of-the-art technology and interdisciplinary expertise to pioneer groundbreaking solutions from inception. Our approach seamlessly blends rigorous scientific research, advanced engineering principles, and unwavering environmental stewardship. The result is a suite of sustainable solutions meticulously crafted to deliver tangible benefits to communities globally.

  • Interdisciplinary Excellence: Our approach combines the best minds from various disciplines, fostering collaboration and innovation. By integrating expertise in chemistry, engineering, and environmental science, we develop holistic solutions that address complex challenges effectively.
  • Sustainability at Core: Sustainability is not just a goal; it's a guiding principle in everything we do. From concept to implementation, we prioritize eco-friendly practices and materials. Our solutions are designed to minimize environmental impact while maximizing long-term benefits for communities and ecosystems.

One of our flagship solutions is the Magnetic Arsenic Removal Unit which utilizes Molecular Magnetic Treatment of Arsenic in a sustainable and cost effective manner (Cost of Water per Litre: Nearly 0.1 Paise per litre ) Highly scalable method suitable for any capacity, ensuring flexibility and design adaptability. High Flow Rate Technology for Arsenic Removal i.e., @800 LPH - ~500ppb, @5000 LPH - ~2000 (As per recorded).
